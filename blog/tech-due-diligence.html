Tech Due Diligence for Investors & Acquirers

May 5, 2025 • AlphaPebble Team

We've productized technical due diligence to help VCs, acquirers, and scale-stage founders audit codebases and infrastructure before committing to a deal.

Who It Helps

  • → Investors looking to derisk funding rounds
  • → Acquirers reviewing technical health
  • → Series A+ founders prepping for diligence

What's Included

  • ✔️ Infra & deployment pipeline teardown - We analyze cloud architecture, deployment processes, and infrastructure costs to identify optimization opportunities and potential scaling issues.
  • ✔️ Code quality & scalability audit - Our team reviews code organization, test coverage, technical debt, and architectural decisions to assess maintainability and scalability.
  • ✔️ Scorecard with red/yellow/green flags - We provide a clear, actionable report highlighting critical issues, moderate concerns, and strengths in the technical foundation.
  • ✔️ Post-sprint recommendations - You'll receive prioritized recommendations for addressing technical debt, improving processes, and enhancing security posture.

Format

1-week sprint · Async + 1 review call

Our technical due diligence process is designed to be minimally disruptive while providing maximum insight. We work directly with the technical team to understand the codebase, infrastructure, and development processes without slowing down their work.

Unlike traditional consulting engagements that can drag on for weeks, our productized approach delivers clear, actionable insights in just one week. This timeline aligns perfectly with the fast-paced nature of investment decisions and acquisition processes.
