CASE STUDY

Demand Forecasting for "UrbanHarvest"

Problem: A D2C grocery startup needed to decide which of 5 new neighborhoods to expand into, but had no historical sales data.

Experiment (7 days): We built a micro-funnel combining hyper-local search ads with a simple waitlist landing page. By analyzing cost-per-signup and demographic signals, we modeled demand.

Outcome: Delivered a ranked list of neighborhoods with a 75% confidence score, saving an estimated $50k in a potential failed launch.

7 Days $50k Saved 75% Confidence
