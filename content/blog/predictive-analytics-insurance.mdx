**Problem Statement:** Legacy risk assessment models relied on static data and manual processes, failing to capture dynamic risk patterns and leading to suboptimal pricing and inaccurate claims prediction, costing the business significant revenue over time.

**Impact:** Through a low-risk, pilot-first validation, our ML-powered risk models improved prediction accuracy by 30%, reduced claims processing time by 40%, and generated $500K in annual savings through better risk pricing, delivering tangible results rapidly and building a sustainable AI culture.
