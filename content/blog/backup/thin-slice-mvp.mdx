---
title: "The 'Thin Slice' MVP: Shipping Value in 30 Days"
subtitle: "Forget building everything. Learn how to identify and ship the smallest possible version of your product that solves a real problem."
tags: ["MVP", "Product Development"]
---

The term 'Minimum Viable Product' is often misused. A 'Thin Slice' MVP delivers a complete, end-to-end solution for one critical user workflow. Build one full vertical slice (UI, backend, data, automation) that solves a single painful problem perfectly; then iterate.
