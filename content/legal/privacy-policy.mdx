<h3 id="information-collection"></h3>
## Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, subscribe to our newsletter, fill out a form, or contact us for support.

### Personal Information

- **Contact Information**: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
- **Professional Information**: Company name, job title, and industry
- **Communication Data**: Messages you send to us and records of our interactions

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, time spent on our site, referral sources, and user interactions
- **Location Data**: General geographic location based on IP address

### Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance your experience and gather information about visitors and visits to our website.

<h3 id="legal-basis"></h3>
## Legal Basis for Processing

As required by **GDPR (Art. 6)** and **DPDP (Sec. 4–6)**, we process your personal data only when we have a lawful basis:

- **Consent**: When you subscribe to newsletters, marketing, or optional features
- **Contractual Necessity**: To provide our services, process payments, and maintain accounts
- **Legal Obligation**: To comply with tax, regulatory, or judicial requirements
- **Legitimate Interests**: To improve services, ensure security, and prevent fraud (balanced with your rights)

You may withdraw consent at any time, without affecting prior lawful processing.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

### Service Provision

- Provide, maintain, and improve our services
- Process transactions and send related information
- Send technical notices and support messages

### Communication

- Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
- Send newsletters and marketing communications (with your consent)

- Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends

> **Note:** We will not process your data in ways incompatible with the original purpose of collection unless you provide fresh consent (DPDP Sec. 5; GDPR Art. 5).

## Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except in the following circumstances:

### Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting business, provided they agree to maintain confidentiality.

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

### Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

### Consent

We may share your information with your explicit consent for specific purposes not covered in this policy.

> **Compliance Note:** Any third parties processing your data act as **Data Processors (GDPR)** or **Data Fiduciaries/Processors (DPDP)** under binding contracts.

<h3 id="data-security"></h3>
## Data Security & Retention

We implement **appropriate technical and organizational security measures** as required by **GDPR Art. 32** and **DPDP Sec. 8**, including:

- **Encryption**: SSL/TLS protection for data transmission
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access and authentication
- **Audits**: Regular security assessments and vulnerability testing
- **Data Minimization**: Collecting only necessary information

### Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

<h3 id="your-rights"></h3>
## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information.

### Under GDPR (EU Residents):

- Right to Access, Rectification, Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”)
- Right to Restrict Processing & Object to Processing
- Right to Data Portability
- Right to Withdraw Consent

### Under DPDP (India Residents):

- Right to Access Information
- Right to Correction and Erasure
- Right to Nominate a representative in case of incapacity or death
- Right to Grievance Redressal

➡️ To exercise these rights, contact us at **privacy@alphapebble.com**.
We will respond within **30 days (GDPR Art. 12) / prescribed timelines (DPDP Sec. 12)**.

## Children’s Privacy
