Artificial Intelligence is everywhere, but for a startup founder, the noise can be deafening. The key isn't to adopt AI for its own sake, but to apply it surgically to solve your most pressing business problems. Begin with a 'Workflow MVP' using off-the-shelf APIs. A small, successful experiment that saves your team 10 hours a week beats a grand AI vision that never ships.
